# Welcome to Jernel

What is Jernel? Well, I'm still figuring that out as I go. It will be some sort of journaling, planning, organizing app. Because the internet doesn't have very many of those yet 🙃!

Seriously though, Jernel is about two things:

## A place to organize my mind

Despite the proliferation of apps for planning, note taking, task management, journaling, outlining, etc, none of them ever quite fit the way that I think or work. I want simple, fun, and infinitely flexible. Is that too much to ask?

I have a bunch of ideas, but I'm not going to make any promises here. We'll see how this unfolds.

## An example of building in public

I just started a blog, [It's Just a Function](https://just-a-function.dev). There are two most important ideas that I would like to share, and this project is one way to help me share both of them. If you browse the commit history of this project, hopefully you'll find that I'm doing both of these consistently.

**1. Build in Tiny Steps**
Working in tiny steps, a couple functions at a time, is often the easiest way to build something. It's low stress, and low risk.

**2. Build in Public**
Share your thinking, and your progress, early and often. It doesn't have to be polished or performative. Just put it out there. In tiny steps. That will help you express your ideas, commit to actually shipping things, and get over the urge to hold onto things until they're perfect.
